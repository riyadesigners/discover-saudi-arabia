Requirements For a Saudi e-Visa From India

E-Visa

A 12-month, multiple-entry, tourist E-Visa that allows visitors to stay in Saudi for 90 days.

  • Includes tourism activities and Umrah (except Hajj season)
  • Excludes other activities such as business and studies
  • Visitors belonging to 49 countries from North America, Europe, Asia, and Oceania are eligible. Click here for a complete list of countries.

Visa Update for GCC Residents* Foreign nationals who are residing in one of the GCC countries can obtain an E-Visa.

  • The visa is valid for both Umrah and tourism.
  • The visa allows them to enjoy a stay of up to 3 months in Saudi Arabia.
  • The applicants are required to hold a passport that is valid for 6 months.
  • Those who are holding a minimum 3 months valid GCC countries residence permit visa are eligible to get the tourist E-Visa for Saudi Arabia.
Visa-On-Arrival

12-month tourism Visa-on-Arrival It’s available for visitors from countries that are eligible for the E-Visa.

    US, UK, and Schengen Visa holders are also eligible, given they meet the following criteria:
  • The US, UK, or Schengen visa must be valid and used at least once with an entry stamp from the issuing country.
  • The US, UK, or Schengen visa must be either a business or tourist visa.

Before You Travel E-Visa Requirements

  • Color Copy of Valid Passport
  • Color Visa Copy (US/UK/SCHENGEN or GCC countries)
  • White Background Photograph
