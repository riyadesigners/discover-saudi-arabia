mmerse in Saudi’s Rich Culture and Heritage

Explore like a LocalGo beyond the deserts

Wallow in the deep-rooted culture of the Arabs and Islamic heritage. Its long-standing traditions, history-rich museums, preserved heritages, savoury spices, delectable cuisines, and other hidden secrets will make you fall in love with this Middle Eastern Kingdom.

Palaces

Stride like a royal through the majestic palaces and fortresses of Saudi. Steeped in history, Diriyah Palace, Qasr Ibrahim Palace, and many more stand gracefully exuding regality today.

Museums

Travel through time to trace the footprints of Arabs and how the Arabian land came into existence. Flock to the National Museum of Saudi Arabia, King Abdulaziz Center For World Culture, Tayabat Museum, and more to explore Saudi’s rich history.

Souqs

Besides desert, safaris and palaces, Saudi is a paradise for shoppers. Old bazaars also known as souqs offer everything from savoury spices to beautiful handicrafts. Shop till you drop at Al-Qaisariya Souq, Handicraft Souq, Al-Dira Souk, Souq Al Zal and more.

Cuisines

When it comes to cuisines, Saudi is a carnival for your taste buds. From zesty to sweet dishes, its palatial bouquet of cuisines will satiate all your Middle Eastern cravings. Make sure to try Umm Ali, Mamool, Matabaq, Kabsa, Qahwa, and more.

Heritage

Step into the old Saudi that continues to hold on to the undertones of traditional Arabian land. Visit Ushaiqer Heritage Village, Al Qasab Village, Al Shuara Heritage Village, Al Muftaha Village, and more. 

Hafawah

Hafawah means being generous, hospitable, and kind-hearted. It is often spoken about through practices, poems, and stories that come from the Kingdom of Saudi. Sometimes the doors are left open to show the home owner’s readiness to receive any visitor at any given time!
